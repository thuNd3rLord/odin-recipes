Ramen

Description

Kickstart your day or boost up it with the ramen

Any Naruto Fans??? We got your back !!!

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles

  • Garlic and Ginger

  • Chicken Broth

  • Dried Shiitake Mushrooms

  • Carrots or Kale

  • Egg and Chilli Oil

Steps

  1. Stir-Fry garlic and ginger

  2. Add chicken broth and dried shiitake mushrooms

  3. Cook the noodles right in the broth

  4. Add thinly sliced kale, shredded carrots

  5. Add a soft-boiled egg, chili oil, hot sauce, etc.
