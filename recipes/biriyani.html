Biriyani

Description

Tasty chicken biriyani you have never tasted with the special secret masala flavour added

Come and get your dishes within 30 minutes !!!

Ingredients

  • Basmathi Rice

  • Chicken Pieces

  • Masala

  • Coconut Oil

  • Coconut Milk

Steps

  1. Make rice

  2. Mix masala with chicken

  3. Fry the chicken

  4. Pour water and coconut milk into the fried chicken

  5. Add the rice finally

  6. Mix them up

  7. It's Ready !!!
