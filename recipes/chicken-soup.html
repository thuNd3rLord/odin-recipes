Chicken Soup

Description

All you need is a chicken soup and then another bowl of it

We bet you get another one cause one bowl isn't enough !

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces

  • Carrots, Celery, and an Onion

  • Salt, Pepper, and Chicken Bouillon Granules

Steps

  1. Make the stock by boiling the chicken and veggies until the meat is falling off the bone

  2. Remove the chicken and cut into pieces

  3. Strain the vegetables and cut into pieces

  4. Return everything to the pot

  5. Heat the soup through and season to tast
